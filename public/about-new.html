2008
Small startup
Starting as a small business in 2008 in the US, we now have big dreams. Initially, the startup was helping friends and relatives order goods from the US. It all started in a small apartment of youngsters trying to source products for their loved ones outside the US. However, things started to get serious with the website launch and below-the-line advertising. We began attracting clients from different parts of the world.
2023
Large-scale business
This is where we realized that it’s not a small business anymore and could be expanded beyond imagination because our service was in demand. We registered our company and rented a warehouse to maintain our quality service of assisting international shoppers in shopping from the USA despite border restraints. Two decades later, Dester has become a large-scale business with hubs in the USA, UK, Germany, and UAE.

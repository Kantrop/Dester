Full text of the policy

What are cookies?

Like most other online companies, we use cookies which are small data files placed on your computer, tablet, or mobile phone (hereafter in the generic sense — «device»). Cookies store certain information each time you visit our websites, use our services, applications, tools, click on our ads, and exchange messages with us. Certain names and types of cookies and similar technologies that we use may change with time.

To let you get a clearer idea of the company’s policy in relation to the use of cookies and similar technologies, below is a short glossary of basic terms.

Cookies

Cookies — small text files (usually consisting of letters and numbers) stored in the memory of your web browser or device when you visit our website or view a message from us. These files allow the website to identify certain types of devices and web browsers.

The following types of cookies exist:

  • session cookies that stop functioning once an web browser session is over and allow tracking your actions during the session;
  • persistent cookies that are stored on your device between web browser sessions and remember your preferences and actions when visiting a number of different websites; own cookies set by the website you are visiting; third-party cookies set by third-party websites separately from the one you are visiting.

Cookies can be disabled or deleted by built-in tools of most commercial web browsers. Each web browser has its own preference settings, and also different web browsers offer different functional capabilities or settings options.

Similar technologies for information storage

Similar technologies for information storage — technologies allowing to record certain information into web browser’s cache memory or device memory using local shared objects or locally stored data, such as Flash and HTML 5 cookies and other elements of applied software for the Internet. These technologies can be used in all web browsers. In some cases, using locally stored data through browsers is not available to the full extent and requires certain tools. Such data storage technologies can be used by us to protect the data of your personal account or to identify unusual activity when using a browser in order to prevent unauthorized access to your account or assess operational efficiency of our websites, services, applications, and tools. We do not use these technologies for targeted advertisement on our or any other websites. The terms «cookies» and «other technologies» are interchangeable in our policies and stand for all technologies that can be used by us to record data into web browser’s cache memory or device memory to collect data helping to identify you in accordance with the above-mentioned provisions.

Your choice when cookies and similar technologies are used by us

We offer users of our websites a number of functions, services, applications, and tools available only when using the above-mentioned technologies. You can always block, disable or delete cookies and other similar technologies, if your web browser or device allow doing it. However, in this case you probably will not be able to use some of the functions of our websites as well as some services, applications, and tools. Also, you will probably have to enter your password more often during your session on our websites. Usually these technologies allow our websites, services, applications, and tools to store corresponding information in web browser’s cache memory or device memory and scan it later. We do not record your personal data into these files, we only use them to identify you on our servers and in our internal systems. If possible, we try to protect our cookies and similar technologies so that they are used only by us and/or our authorized service providers. For this reason, we give them unique identification numbers. None of our cookies contains any personal data of our users. We ask for your permission to use the above-mentioned technologies to collect and store information openly notifying you of their use and giving you a chance to stop using these technologies at your discretion, as described above.

We use the above-mentioned technologies for the following purposes:

  1. Operational needs. We can use cookies and similar technologies to ensure proper operation of our websites, services, applications, and tools. For example, they can be used to provide your access to our websites, services, applications, and tools in order to prevent violator activity and to improve security or to let you use such functions as online cart, saving search results, etc.
  2. Working efficiency. We can use cookies and similar technologies to assess the efficiency of our websites, services, applications, and tools, including for the purposes of analytical research, to find out how exactly our users use our websites as well as to improve their content and to perfect our applications, services, and tools.
  3. Extending functionality. We can use cookies and similar technologies to offer you a wider range of functional capabilities when using our websites, services, applications, and tools. For instance, they can be used to identify you when registering on our websites and to track your preferences, interests, and viewed pages in order to improve the provided content.
  4. Advertising and targeted audience outreach. We can use cookies and similar technologies to provide content, including adverts adapted to your interests, on our and other websites. For example, they can be used to identify the relevance of the adverts shown to you and content provided.
